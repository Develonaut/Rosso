Our Story

Our pizzeria opened in 2007. Kevin Cronin & John Franchetti dreamed of opening a pizzeria that reflected the Italian country side but added a the small intimiate shop feeling, where food is created from scratch from local and seasonal ingredients. Inspired by the slow food movement of Italy and local Sonoma County farmers, Rosso was born. Our Tuscan wood-burning oven gives you the true Neapolitan style of crisp, yeasty pizza.making Rosso' a true handcrafted and loved pizza experience.
